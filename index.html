Our Story

Born from a passion for authentic Kerala flavors, Nadan Curry Chatti brings you the true taste of God's Own Country

2022 - The Beginning

Started with a dream to bring authentic Kerala home-style cooking to food lovers. Our founders, passionate about preserving traditional recipes, opened the first Nadan Curry Chatti.

2023 - Recognition

Gained recognition as the most authentic Kerala restaurant in the city. Featured in local food magazines and won the "Best Traditional Cuisine" award.

2024 - Expansion

Expanded our menu to include more regional specialties and introduced online ordering. Now serving over 500 satisfied customers weekly.

2025 - Future

Continuing our mission to serve the most authentic Kerala cuisine while maintaining the homely touch that makes us special.
